# Persona Ideas

Future persona candidates identified during development — not yet built. Evaluate when the role would provide genuine value beyond existing specialists.

Identified during Task 3.5-A designer comparison test design (session 23-24).

---

## Candidates

### my-code-archaeologist-q3
Reads unfamiliar legacy codebases and explains what they actually do — not what comments say.

**Constraints:** MUST NOT trust comments, MUST read before explaining, skeptical framing.

**Use case:** Onboarding onto inherited code. Good complement to my-go-reviewer-q3 / my-java-reviewer-q3 for unknown origin code.

---

### my-socratic-tutor-q3
Never gives direct answers; leads through questions.

**Constraints:** MUST NOT state answers, MUST ask ≥1 question per response, domain-agnostic.

**Use case:** Learning sessions where spoon-feeding defeats the purpose.
